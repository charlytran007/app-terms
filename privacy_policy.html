Your Privacy Matters

At Multi-Seller Connector, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and security. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

Multi-Seller Connector ("we," "our," or "us") provides a data integration platform that connects e-commerce platforms for unified analytics and reporting. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our service.

By using our service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, billing information
  • Authentication Credentials: Passwords, API keys, OAuth tokens for connected platforms
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to our support team, feedback, and survey responses

2.2 Data from Connected Platforms

When you authorize us to connect to your TikTok Shop, Shopee, or other e-commerce accounts, we collect:

Data Category Examples Purpose
Order Data Order IDs, order status, amounts, timestamps, shipping info Analytics, reporting, sync to BigQuery
Product Data Product names, SKUs, prices, descriptions, categories, images Inventory management, analytics
Inventory Data Stock levels, warehouse locations, availability Stock tracking, alerts
Customer Data Customer names, shipping addresses, order history Order fulfillment, analytics
Financial Data Transaction amounts, payment methods, settlement info Financial reporting, reconciliation

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies (see Cookie Policy below)
  • Log Data: Error logs, API request logs, system performance data

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication, principle of least privilege
  • Secure Storage: OAuth tokens and API keys encrypted in secure vaults
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments, vulnerability scanning, penetration testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring, automated threat detection
  • Data Backup: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery procedures

5.2 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Using strong, unique passwords
  • Enabling two-factor authentication when available
  • Notifying us immediately of any security breaches

5.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may affect your information, we will notify you within 72 hours and provide information about the breach and remedial actions.
